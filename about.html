Easy Access

Our Story

JuaKazi was born from a simple observation: talented service providers in Kenya often struggle to connect with customers who need their services. Meanwhile, people waste time searching for reliable professionals they can trust.

Founded in 2024, we set out to bridge this gap by creating a platform that puts trust, quality, and community at its heart. Our name "JuaKazi" means "know work" in Swahili, reflecting our commitment to helping people find skilled professionals who truly know their craft.

Today, we're proud to connect thousands of service providers with customers across Kenya, from bustling Nairobi to rural communities, making quality services accessible to all.
