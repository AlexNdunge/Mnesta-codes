Safety Tips

For Customers

  • ✓ Always book through the JuaKazi platform
  • ✓ Check provider ratings and reviews before booking
  • ✓ Keep communication within the platform
  • ✓ Never share sensitive personal information
  • ✓ Report any suspicious behavior immediately
  • ✓ Leave honest reviews after service completion

For Providers

  • ✓ Maintain professional conduct at all times
  • ✓ Arrive on time for scheduled appointments
  • ✓ Respect customer property and privacy
  • ✓ Communicate clearly about services and pricing
  • ✓ Report any safety concerns to JuaKazi
  • ✓ Keep your profile and availability updated
